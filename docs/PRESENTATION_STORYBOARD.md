## üé® Visual Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep blue/purple gradients (trust, intelligence)
- **Accent:** Bright green for positive metrics (money saved)
- **Alert:** Red for risk (but not too aggressive)
- **Background:** Dark theme (professional, data-focused)

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold, sans-serif (Poppins, Inter, or SF Pro)
- **Metrics:** Extra bold, large size for impact
- **Body:** Clean, readable (avoid walls of text)

### Transitions
- Smooth fades (not jarring cuts)
- Pan/zoom on dashboards for emphasis
- Animated typing for GenAI explanations (shows "AI thinking")

### Screen Recording Tips
- **High resolution:** 1920x1080 minimum
- **Cursor highlights:** Circle cursor for visibility
- **Smooth scrolling:** Slow, deliberate movements
- **No loading delays:** Pre-load all pages before recording

## üèÜ Judge Psychology

### What Judges Want to See

**Technical Judges:**
- Proof the AI actually works (validation results)
- Evidence of ML learning (pattern discovery)
- Production readiness signals (monitoring, feedback loops)

**Business Judges:**
- Clear ROI ($$$)
- Realistic problem (not contrived)
- Scalable solution (not one-off hack)

**All Judges:**
- **Confidence:** You believe in this solution
- **Clarity:** Easy to understand, even for non-experts
- **Completeness:** End-to-end story, not just a model

### How to Stand Out

1. **Lead with business impact, not tech specs**
   - Bad: "We built a HistGradientBoostingClassifier with..."
   - Good: "$32M problem, 5x ROI solution"

2. **Show, don't tell**
   - Bad: "The AI generates explanations"
   - Good: [Live demo of GenAI typing explanation]

3. **Be honest about synthetic data**
   - Bad: Pretend it's real
   - Good: "Synthetic data, stress-tested for production reality"

4. **End with a punch**
   - Last thing judges remember is the closing number
   - Make it memorable: "5x ROI. $25M incremental value. Predict. Explain. Defend."
