## ğŸ¨ DESIGN GUIDELINES

### Color Palette
- **Background:** Dark (#0e1117)
- **Text:** White (#ffffff)
- **Accent 1:** Blue (#4299e1) - trust
- **Accent 2:** Green (#48bb78) - success/money
- **Accent 3:** Red (#e53e3e) - risk/alerts

### Typography
- **Headline:** 48-60pt, bold
- **Body:** 24-32pt, regular
- **Numbers:** 72pt+, extra bold
- **Font:** Inter, Poppins, or SF Pro Display

### Animations
- Simple fade-in (300ms)
- No fancy transitions (distracting)
- Build bullet points (not all at once)
